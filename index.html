Tournament Days

All round robin games fit within the first (n-1) days. The last day is always semifinals and finals. We try to spread games evenly – teams will never have more than max games per day.

Finals will be placed as close to this slot as possible. Older brackets (U18) get the slot first when capacity is tight; younger brackets overflow one slot earlier.

Advanced Scheduling Rules
Team Rest Between Games When ON, a minimum 90-minute gap (2 slots) is enforced between a team's games on the same day. Turn OFF to allow back-to-back scheduling when courts are limited.
Enforce Venue Change Rest When ON, a team switching venues on the same day must have at least 180 minutes of actual rest (270 min start-to-start, a 3-slot gap) before tip-off at the new venue — enough time to pack, travel, eat, warm up. Stricter than the 90-min same-venue team rest — may reduce scheduling density on tight court configs.
Mode: Hard constraint: games that cannot satisfy the 3-hour gap will be left unscheduled (shown as failures).
Daily Game Distribution (No Blank Days) When ON, every team must play at least 1 game on every tournament day. Prevents wasted travel days for teams flying in for the whole event. Acts as a safety rail — for the default 8/12/16-team mix this is already satisfied by other rules, but the constraint catches regressions if the configuration changes.
Mode: Hard constraint: if any team would have a blank day, generation fails and the conflict is reported so you can soften the rule.
