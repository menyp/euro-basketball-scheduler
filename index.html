Tournament Days

All round robin games fit within the first (n-1) days. The last day is always semifinals and finals. We try to spread games evenly – teams will never have more than max games per day.

Advanced Scheduling Rules

Toggle rules OFF to relax constraints when games fail to schedule. Disabled rules show amber warnings instead of blocking placement.

Enforce Blanes Exclusivity (U16/U18 Boys) ON: Boys U16/U18 are placed at Blanes courts only — a red error is shown if Blanes is full. OFF: Blanes is preferred but any court is used as fallback, shown as an amber warning.
Enforce Club Staff Overlap Protection ON: teams from the same club are kept in different time slots where possible. OFF: club conflicts are ignored entirely to free up slots for placement.
Enforce 90-Minute Team Rest ON: a team must skip at least one full 90-min slot between games. OFF: back-to-back slots are allowed and flagged with an amber warning.
